About

Emilia Podmore

Junior Developer, based in London

Transitioning into tech has enabled me to pursue my passion for coding. Through SheCodes, I developed a range of technical skills and continue to expand my knowledge. I enjoy creating innovative, user-friendly websites that combine creativity with my media background.

As someone who is AuDHD, I approach challenges with a methodical mindset, using attention to detail to strengthen problem-solving and build systematic solutions. I am also dedicated to representing equity and accessibility in tech.

My aim is to tackle misinformation surrounding Autism and ADHD through developing a website that contains inclusive content with evidence-based research and support from disability professionals and advocates.
